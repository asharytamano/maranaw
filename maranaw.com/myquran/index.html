About this App

App Name: myQur'an

Short Description: Arabic text with Maranao, Tagalog, Bisaya, and English translations.

Long Description: myQur’an is an ad-free Qur’an reader. It presents the Arabic Uthmani script alongside Maranao, Tagalog, Bisaya, and English translations. Choose your preferred reciter and play a single ayah or an entire surah. Clear typography, dark mode, search, bookmarks, and reliable offline access. No sign-ups, no trackers—just reading. Forever free, fi sabeelillah!
